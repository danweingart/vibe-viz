Design System / Share Button

Share Button

Unified dropdown for download and social sharing

Closed State

Default state. Shows share icon, label, and chevron indicator.

Open State (Dropdown Visible)

Chevron rotates 180deg. Dropdown positioned right-aligned below button.

Loading State (Downloading)

Icon replaced with spinner. Both options disabled during loading.
